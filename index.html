About Eahika

Where Ideas Meet Action

Eahika — meaning "coming together" — is a nonprofit organization rooted in the belief that rigorous scholarship, applied wisdom, and community engagement can create meaningful change.

We work across disciplines, bringing together researchers, practitioners, educators, and communities to co-produce knowledge that matters. Our work spans public health, climate change, governance, social inequalities, and more.

At Eahika, every idea is a seed. We cultivate them through research, grow them through learning, and apply them through partnership and public engagement.
