Ongoing Projects

Ongoing

Community Health Resilience in Peri-Urban Areas

Investigating community health infrastructure gaps and co-designing interventions with local health workers across five peri-urban districts of West Bengal.


📍 West Bengal 🗓 2024–2026
Ongoing

Climate Adaptation Strategies in the Sundarbans

Participatory action research documenting local knowledge and adaptive practices in Sundarban communities facing rising sea levels and cyclone frequency.


📍 Sundarbans, WB 🗓 2023–2025
Ongoing

Urban Governance and Waste Workers' Rights

Ethnographic and policy research on the conditions, rights, and labor organization of urban sanitation workers in Kolkata's municipal system.


📍 Kolkata 🗓 2024–2025
Ongoing

Digital Equity and Technology Access Study

📍 Eastern India 🗓 2024–ongoing

Flagship Programs

Research Initiatives

Beyond individual projects, Eahika runs several long-term research initiatives that anchor our thematic commitments and create sustained engagement with key communities and issues.

Initiative

Eahika Archive Project

A long-term initiative to document, preserve, and make accessible community histories, oral testimonies, and grassroots knowledge from across Eastern and Northeast India.

Initiative

Climate Justice Lab

An interdisciplinary research lab examining climate change through the lens of social justice — connecting environmental science with social analysis and community voice.

Initiative

Public Policy Observatory

Ongoing monitoring and analysis of key public policy developments in health, education, environment, and governance — producing accessible briefs for public and policy audiences.

Initiative

Community Knowledge Hub

A participatory platform that supports community organizations in documenting, analyzing, and mobilizing their own knowledge for advocacy and self-determination.
